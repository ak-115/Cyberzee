What We Do

Our Past Events

A look back at what we've accomplished together.

May 2024

Cyberthon '24

Our flagship 24-hour CTF event, featuring over 50 teams from various universities. Challenges covered web exploitation, reverse engineering, cryptography, and digital forensics.

March 2024

Intro to Wi-Fi Hacking

A hands-on workshop where participants learned the fundamentals of wireless network security, packet sniffing, and common vulnerabilities in WPA/WPA2 protocols.

January 2024

Guest Lecture: A Career in Pentesting

We hosted a senior security analyst from a leading cybersecurity firm who shared insights on building a career in ethical hacking and the skills required to succeed.
